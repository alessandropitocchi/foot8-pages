How to join

Partecipa al gruppo → Join the group →

After joining the group, use the link below to download Foot8 from the Play Store. Use the same Google account you joined the group with.

Scarica da Play Store (anteprima) → Accesso diretto al test

Note: if the Play Store link doesn't show the app immediately, wait a few minutes after joining the group and try again.
